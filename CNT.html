Tunable Performance in Application 可調控的應用表現

From Transparent to Highly Conductive 從高透光到高導電，精準控制

This CNT dispersion is designed to be diluted and formulated into a final coating. The examples show a coating with 0.5 wt.% CNT on an insulative PET film. By using different Wire Bar Coaters, which control the wet film thickness, the final properties can be precisely engineered. 此分散液可被稀釋或與樹脂調配成最終塗料。圖例展示了將含有0.5wt.% CNT的塗料，應用於絕緣PET薄膜上的結果。透過使用不同的線棒塗佈器 (Wire Bar Coater) 來控制濕膜厚度，最終的性能可以被精準地設計。

A lower RDS number (e.g., No. 2) deposits a thinner film, resulting in a highly transparent coating with a sheet resistance of 10⁶ Ohm/sq—ideal for anti-static applications where clarity is key. A higher RDS number (e.g., No. 7) deposits a thicker film, achieving a more conductive 10⁴ Ohm/sq for applications requiring superior ESD protection. 較低的RDS號數（如 No.2）會產生較薄的塗層，得到高透明度、表面電阻為 10⁶ Ohm/sq 的成品，是透明度至關重要的防靜電應用的理想選擇。較高的RDS號數（如 No.7）則會產生較厚的塗層，達到導電性更佳的 10⁴ Ohm/sq，適用於需要更強ESD靜電防護的場景。

A Deeper Look at Our Specifications 規格深度解析

Low Viscosity低黏度 (<50 mPa·s)
Indicates excellent flowability. This is crucial for achieving smooth, uniform coatings using various industrial methods without requiring complex equipment adjustments.代表了優異的流動性。這對於使用各種工業方法（如噴塗、浸塗）獲得平滑、均勻的塗層至關重要，無需複雜的設備調整。
High Aspect Ratio高長徑比
With a length of 12μm (12,000nm) and a diameter of 13nm, the aspect ratio is nearly 1000:1. This allows the CNTs to form interconnected conductive pathways efficiently, even at low concentrations.長度為12μm（12,000nm），直徑為13nm，長徑比接近1000:1。這使得奈米碳管即使在低濃度下也能有效地形成相互連接的導電路徑。
High Carbon Purity高碳素純度 (96%)
A high purity level means fewer impurities like amorphous carbon or metallic catalysts, which can hinder electrical performance and cause defects in the final product. 96% is a high-grade standard ensuring reliable and consistent conductivity.高純度意味著非晶碳或金屬催化劑等雜質含量較低，這些雜質會妨礙電氣性能並在最終產品中造成缺陷。96%是一個高規格標準，確保了可靠且一致的導電性。
High Concentration高濃度 (5wt.%)
Providing a high-concentration masterbatch gives formulators maximum flexibility. It can be easily diluted to a precise target concentration (like the 0.5 wt.% in the example) for optimal cost-performance.提供高濃度的母液給予配方設計師最大的靈活性。它可以輕易地被稀釋到精確的目標濃度（如示例中的0.5 wt.%），以達到最佳的成本效益。
